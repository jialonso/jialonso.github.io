About me

I received the MSc. and PhD degrees in Computer Science in 2009 and 2016, respectively, from the University of Castilla-La Mancha, Spain. I am currently member of the Intelligent Systems and Datamining research group in the Albacete Research Institute of Informatics I3A.

I am interested in learning from data within machine learning, artificial intelligence and statistics. I am particularly interested in learning and reasoning with probabilistic graphical models such as Bayesian networks.

I work at Grupo Vermon and I am currently involved in various projects related to IoT and Big Data.
